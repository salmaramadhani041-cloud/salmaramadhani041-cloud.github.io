About My Hobby

Haiking

For me, hiking is not just a hobby but a way for me to continue to grow into a person who appreciates nature more. I feel safe when I am in nature. Nature is so beautiful for me. Nature teaches me various things.

My hobby is climbing because it challenges both my physical strength and mental focus. I enjoy the excitement of reaching new heights, exploring nature, and pushing myself beyond my limits. Climbing also teaches me patience, confidence, and problem-solving skills while helping me stay active and healthy
